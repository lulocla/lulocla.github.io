External Placement

Optional  |  5 ECTS  |  Code: M0.166  |  18 Feb 2026 – 1 Jul 2026

Professor: Antoni Pérez Navarro

About this Subject

The External Placement module is an optional curricular placement equivalent to 125 hours of work. It allows students to apply their bioinformatics and biostatistics knowledge in a real professional context, gaining practical experience during their training. Placements can be carried out online or in person, with the company and student agreeing on the modality. Students must have completed 30 ECTS of the master's before enrolling. A placement supervisor and an academic tutor provide personalised support throughout.

Contents
  • The content of the placement is determined by the partner company and its ongoing projects at the time of the student's placement.
  • Students may find their own placement company or choose from those offered by UOC — all require a formal agreement managed through the virtual campus.
Skills Gained
  • Applied bioinformatics and biostatistics in a professional setting
  • Adapting efficiently to new work environments and tools
  • Understanding the organisation and strategic direction of a research company or centre
  • Developing an individual project with defined objectives and learning outcomes
  • Professional scientific communication and reporting
