Who am I?

I am a Spanish fourth-year biomedical science student at Abertay University, Dundee, Scotland. My interest in biomedical science grew from wanting to understand our bodies. It's the only one we get from birth to death, therefore taking care of it is crucial for survival. From then on, I started learning about metabolism and how our bodies stay balanced. As I learned more about biomedical science, I got really interested in cancer research, nutrigenetics and metabolic and endocrine disorders. Feel free to explore my website to learn more about me, my background, and my aspirations!
