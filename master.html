About the Programme

The Master's degree in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics is a joint interuniversity programme offered by the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC) and the Universitat de Barcelona (UB). The programme is designed for professionals working at the intersection of biology, data science, and computational analysis — providing a combined and balanced training in both bioinformatics and biostatistics, two fields that are increasingly critical in modern healthcare and research.

The use of technology in molecular biology, medicine, veterinary science, and agronomy generates enormous volumes of biological and medical data. This master's programme trains specialists in the handling, analysis, and interpretation of these data — a profile highly sought after by companies, universities, and research centres.

The programme is fully online, flexible, and officially accredited by the AQU. View the official programme page.
