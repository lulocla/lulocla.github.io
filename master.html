Master in Bioinformatics & Biostatistics

Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC) & Universitat de Barcelona (UB)  |  Starting September 2025

About the Programme

The Master's degree in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics is a joint interuniversity programme offered by the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC) and the Universitat de Barcelona (UB). The programme is designed for professionals working at the intersection of biology, data science, and computational analysis — providing a combined and balanced training in both bioinformatics and biostatistics, two fields that are increasingly critical in modern healthcare and research.

The use of technology in molecular biology, medicine, veterinary science, and agronomy generates enormous volumes of biological and medical data. This master's programme trains specialists in the handling, analysis, and interpretation of these data — a profile highly sought after by companies, universities, and research centres.

The programme is officially accredited by the AQU (Agència per a la Qualitat del Sistema Universitari de Catalunya). View the official programme page.

Programme Structure — 60 ECTS
Compulsory Modules (30 ECTS)
  • Software for Data Analysis (5 ECTS)
  • Statistical Inference (5 ECTS)
  • Regression, Models and Methods (5 ECTS)
  • Computational Genomics (5 ECTS)
  • Omics Data Analysis (5 ECTS)
  • Programming for Bioinformatics (5 ECTS)
Optional Modules (15 ECTS)
  • Computational Tools for Bioinformatics (5 ECTS)
  • Design and Analysis of Experiments (5 ECTS)
  • External Placement (5 ECTS)
Master's Final Project (15 ECTS)

An individual synthesis project integrating the knowledge and skills acquired throughout the programme, concluded with a virtual, synchronous defence before an evaluation panel.

About This Master?
  • Combined focus: Unique balance of bioinformatics and biostatistics — not one at the expense of the other.
  • Tools: Core languages are R and Python — the industry and research standards for statistical analysis and machine learning.
  • Ranked: UOC is ranked as the best online university in Spain by the CYD 2025 ranking, and among the top 7% of universities worldwide (CWUR 2025).
  • Official title: Verified by the Consejo de Universidades and authorised by the Generalitat de Catalunya, with full academic validity across Spain and the European Higher Education Area.
Career Opportunities

Graduates can work in pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, the food industry, research centres, hospitals, and chemical or biocomputing laboratories, developing profiles such as:

  • Quantitative expert in bioinformatics and biostatistics.
  • Statistical analyst of bioinformatics problems.
  • Data analyst / scientist with expertise in health data and computational tools.
Key Competencies
  • Apply knowledge and solve problems in bioinformatics and biostatistics environments.
  • Research, develop, and innovate in technology centres, universities, and companies.
  • Manage, interpret, and analyse large volumes of data using statistical software (R, Python).
  • Apply machine learning and statistical inference methods in a bioinformatics context.
  • Understand and apply experimental design, omics data analysis, and sequence alignment algorithms.
  • Communicate conclusions clearly to both specialist and non-specialist audiences.
  • Work independently and in multidisciplinary, international teams.
