About the Placement

As part of my BSc Applied Biomedical Science degree at Abertay University, I completed a 6-month clinical laboratory placement at NHS Fife in Blood Sciences and Blood Transfusion. This placement provided invaluable hands-on experience working in a live NHS diagnostic laboratory environment, bridging academic learning with professional clinical practice.
